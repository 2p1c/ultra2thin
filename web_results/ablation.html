Conclusion

The Transformer is unnecessary and slightly harmful for 1D dense prediction. Removing it: (1) cuts parameters 3.7× (1.77M → 476K), (2) improves MAE 40% (0.048 → 0.029mm), (3) trains faster (no O(N²) self-attention on 64 points).

Why: With a dense 64-point grid and kernel-5 Conv1d, the spatial context (±8 cells after 4 layers) is sufficient for thickness prediction. The Transformer's global attention adds trainable variance without providing information that the Conv1d decoder can't already extract from the dense, regular measurement grid.

Recommendation: Drop the Transformer for 1D COMSOL cross-section training. Use SignalEncoder + GeometryEncoder → Conv1d decoder directly.
